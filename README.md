# Rpi_Light_Control

Lazy to turn off the light far from my bed,...

## Requires

- Python3
- Rpi.GPIO
- Flask

## Circut
- Your physical light switch: GND ---- PORT8
- Your Relay: PORT11

## What Can It do?

- Open webpage `http://$IP/light` to see and control light status
- Also can use your physical switch
